Top three activities to do in NYC

Visit Central Park

Discover the natural beauty and serenity of Central Park, an urban oasis offering scenic walking paths, boating, and picnicking spots amidst the city's hustle and bustle.

Explore The Met

Immerse yourself in art, history, and culture at The Met, one of the largest and most renowned art museums in the world, featuring over two million works of art spanning 5,000 years.

See a Broadway Show

Experience the magic of a Broadway show in the Theater District, where world-class productions and performances captivate audiences and bring stories to life in the heart of NYC.
